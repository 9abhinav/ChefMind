How to Make Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g dried pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon salt (for boiling water)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes (crushed or whole)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil (or a handful of fresh basil)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Boil the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente, usually around 8-12 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Once cooked, drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water, and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn it. Stir in the canned tomatoes (crushed or whole) and dried basil. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. If the sauce is too thick, add a little reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.

Step 3: Combine

Add the drained pasta to the sauce in the skillet. Toss well to coat the pasta with the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, add more pasta water for a saucier consistency. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
