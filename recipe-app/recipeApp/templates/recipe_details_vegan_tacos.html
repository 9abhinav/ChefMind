Vegan Tacos

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the black beans, corn, diced red bell pepper, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes until heated through.

Step 2: Warm the Tortillas

While the filling is cooking, warm the corn tortillas in a separate skillet for about 30 seconds on each side, or until soft and pliable.

Step 3: Assemble the Tacos

To each tortilla, add a generous scoop of the filling. Top with diced avocado, red onion, and fresh cilantro.

Step 4: Serve

Serve the tacos with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the top.
